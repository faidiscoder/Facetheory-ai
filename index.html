If you enter health or fitness-related details, we use that information only to generate your overall rating and progress insights inside the app.

If you purchase a subscription, payments are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive or store your full credit card number.

We may collect limited diagnostic and usage data to improve performance and reliability, such as crash reports and app version information.

2) How We Use Your Information

  • To create and secure accounts (email verification)

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information only in the following cases:

We retain information only as long as necessary to operate the Service and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion at any time.

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure.

FaceTheory AI is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.
